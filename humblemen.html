Style Notes

01

Fit First, Always

No fabric, brand, or price point matters more than fit. A £30 shirt that fits perfectly looks better than a £300 shirt that doesn't.

02

Invest in Shoes & Outerwear

These are the two things people see most. Quality footwear and a single excellent coat will elevate any outfit underneath.

03

Neutral Foundation

Build around navy, grey, white, and camel. These work with everything. Add one or two signature colours — not five.

04

Grooming Completes the Look

Clothes carry you only so far. A clean haircut, well-maintained skin, and good posture are non-negotiable finishing details.

05

Buy Less, Choose Well

Before purchasing, ask: does this work with at least three things I already own? If not, leave it. A curated wardrobe is a calm one.

06

Dress for Where You're Going

Context matters. Dressing slightly above the occasion's baseline is confidence. Dressing for the previous version of yourself is nostalgia.
